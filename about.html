About Elegance African Hair Braiding

Where culture, care, and creativity come together.

Our Story

My name is Esse, and I grew up in West Africa learning how to braid hair from braiding school. Hair braiding was an important part of my culture, and I developed my skills at a young age.

As a young adult, I moved to the United States to find better opportunities. It was challenging at first, but I began braiding hair for friends and neighbors. As more people heard about my talent, my client base grew.

After saving money and working hard, I opened my own salon. Today, my business not only provides beautiful hairstyles but also empowers women and celebrates my cultural roots.
